Why Dubbo?

{{% /blocks/lead %}} {{< blocks/section id="oceanNodes" >}} {{% blocks/feature image="framework" url="./overview/what/advantages/usability/" %}} #### [Easy To Use](#), Let Developers Focus On Real Business Unified microservice development paradigm with multi-language SDK support. Supporting any protocol from HTTP/2, gRPC, REST, Thrift, to TCP. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature image="governance" icon="fas fa-share-alt" url="./overview/what/advantages/traffic-management/" %}} #### [Service Governance](#), Monitor and Control Cluster In Real-time Built-in traffic management policies such as service discovery, load balancing, and routing.
